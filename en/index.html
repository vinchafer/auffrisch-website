Our stance

Representation.
Not optimisation.

Many solid Swiss companies have a digital presence that doesn't show what the firm really is. A homepage that hasn't been touched in years. A design you quietly apologise for when you share the URL.

This isn't a question of search engines or click-through rates. It's about how future employees perceive your company before they get in touch. How business partners and suppliers assess your presence. Whether your website confirms what your reputation promises.

We work with companies that have built something — and deserve a presence that shows it clearly.

Frequently asked questions

What you want to know

Clear answers, no technical jargon.

What happens to my old website?
+
Your old website stays online throughout the entire process. We build the new version in parallel — and only once you're completely satisfied do we make the switch. No downtime, no risk.
What do I need to get started?
+
Just a short conversation with us. If you have texts and images — great. If not, we'll help you. Logo, colours and style are discussed together. No technical knowledge required.
Can I edit the website myself afterwards?
+
Yes. On request, we set up a simple system that lets you change texts and images yourself — without any technical knowledge. Alternatively, we can handle maintenance for CHF 990 per year.
Are 14 working days really realistic?
+
Yes — provided we receive your texts and images promptly. The most common reason for longer projects is waiting for client materials. That's why we agree from the start on what comes when.
What does it really cost — are there ongoing fees?
+
The fixed price for the website is all you pay — no hourly rates, no surprise invoices. Additionally, domain and hosting come to around CHF 100–200 per year. We'll cover this transparently in our first conversation. The optional annual maintenance is CHF 990 — but is entirely optional.
Which industries does Auffrisch work with?
+
We work with all local Swiss SMEs: tradespeople, therapists, restaurants, retail, hairdressers, service providers and many more. The more local and personal the business, the greater the impact of a professional website.
How does payment work?
+
You pay after launch — once your website is live and you're satisfied. By invoice or e-banking, no complications.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
+
A full revision round is included in every package — you tell us what needs changing, we implement it. Our goal is for you to be completely satisfied before we close the project.
